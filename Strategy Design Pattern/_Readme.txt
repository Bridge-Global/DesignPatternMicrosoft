Strategy Pattern
---------------------

Here we are having a movieTicket shoping class, According the days the rate of the ticket will get changed.
If sunday there will not be any discount
if mondy 10 % discount will be given
IF Tuesday 15% discount will be given

all these discount informations will be stored inside different different strategy classes
according to the days in a week we will be providing the corresponding StrategyObject to the MovieTicketBooking object
